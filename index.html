Foreword

前言

We live, more than ever, on other people's experience. Reviews, guides, patient stories, podcasts, the advice of AI — personal experience is no longer private memory; it circulates day and night as content, evidence, care, and data. This circulation rests on an assumption we rarely examine: that experience can be spoken, heard, and handed over whole.

Yet understanding always begins from a partial position — no one stands inside another's experience. We know more than we can tell; experience keeps a tacit remainder, and language carries only its surface. Understanding was never a handover. It is an ongoing negotiation: guessing, misreading, repairing — until, at some moment, someone says: I think I understand.

We spent long hours with several blind and low-vision friends, asking them to describe their worlds, and translated those descriptions into moving images. Precisely because between seeing and not seeing the impossibility of directly sharing experience cannot be waved away, the labor that understanding requires becomes most clearly visible. They are not the subject of this work but its most honest case: the same negotiation happens in every act of telling and listening — and it will happen between you and these images.

中文前言

我们比任何时代都更依赖他人的经验生活。攻略、测评、患者自述、播客、AI 的建议——个人经验不再只是私人记忆，它以内容、证据、照护和数据的形式昼夜流通。这套流通建立在一个很少被检查的前提上：经验可以被说出、被听懂、被完整地交到另一个人手里。

可是理解永远从一个局部的位置出发——没有人能站进他人的经验内部。我们知道的，多于我们能说出的。所谓理解，从来不是一次交付，而是一场持续的协商：试探、误解、修正——直到某个瞬间，有人说，我好像懂了。

我们与几位视障朋友长谈，请他们描述自己的世界，再把这些描述转译成画面。恰恰因为在看得见与看不见之间，"经验无法直接共享"最无从回避，理解所需要的劳动也最清晰可见。他们不是这件作品的对象，而是它最诚实的例子——同样的协商，发生在每一次讲述与倾听之间，也将发生在你与这些画面之间。

Eleven Films

十一段影像

Eight Voices

八个声道

Eight channels, eight corners of a dark room. The voices never pair with the screens: to see, you walk to an image; to listen, you stand where the channels balance. Every voice ends on the same sentence — the one this work is named after.

八个声道占据黑暗展厅的八个角落，与画面从不配对：看要走近，听要驻足。每个声音都结束在同一句话上——就是这件作品的名字。

On Site

现场

Credits

The captions beside the films are the translators' second readings — not quotations. Some films combine the descriptions of several storytellers. They record how we listened, not what was said.
影像旁的文字是转译者的再读，不是讲述者原话；部分画面由多位讲述者的描述结合而成。它们记录的是我们如何听，而不是他们说了什么。

Medium: multi-screen digital video, 8-channel sound installation · 多屏数字影像，8声道声音装置

With gratitude to the blind and low-vision storytellers who lent this work their worlds. The sound layer is complete in itself: for blind and low-vision visitors it is the entire work, and the images are its translation into light. 谨向出借了自己世界的视障讲述者致谢。声音层本身即完整作品：对视障观众而言它就是全部，画面是它向光的转译。
